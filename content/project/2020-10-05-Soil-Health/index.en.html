Goal: Modeling Soil Environment for Different Agroecosystems

For more specific objectives:

  1. Developing Soil Health Index (Which can be used to define Health of a particular farm land and can be used to determine the vital capacity/yield efficiency of the land for long-term cropping system)

    • We published one article in this objective: Soil Health Gap

  2. Determine the core ecological community (microorganisms). (Though there are a lot of studies on how microbial/biological communities changes under different environment and management practices; but there is still less number of studies on core community which can be used as an indicators for specific soil health condition.

As a first objective:

we are looking for collaborators from all over the world who are actively involved/studying long-term agroecology. The concept is to compile and study the published data to come up with core hypothesis which can correlate ecological data, agronomic management practices to soil health status.
